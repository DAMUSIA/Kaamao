## 3. Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

- 'Personal Data' means any data about an individual who is identifiable by or in relation to such data, including name, phone number, email address, and location information.
- 'Sensitive Personal Data or Information (SPDI)' under the SPDI Rules includes passwords and any personal data that falls under the categories specified in Rule 3 of the SPDI Rules. GullyGig collects passwords (stored in encrypted form) which qualify as SPDI.
- 'Data Fiduciary' means GullyGig, which determines the purpose and means of processing personal data.
- 'Data Principal' or 'User' means you, the individual whose personal data is being processed.
- 'Data Processor' means a third party that processes data on behalf of GullyGig (e.g., Supabase, Vercel).
- 'Platform' means the GullyGig website accessible at gullygig.in.

## 4. Personal Data We Collect

We collect only such data as is necessary for a lawful purpose connected with the functioning of the Platform (data minimisation principle). We collect the following:

### a) Data Provided During Registration:

- Full name (required)
- Phone number — 10-digit Indian mobile number (required). This is your primary identifier.
- Password — encrypted using bcrypt by Supabase Auth. Never stored in plain text. Qualifies as SPDI under the SPDI Rules.
- Email address (optional). If not provided, a synthetic internal identifier of the format phone_XXXXXXXXXX@gullygig.in is generated for authentication only. This identifier is never visible to you or any other user.
- Date of birth (optional, via profile settings)
- Gender (optional)

### b) Data Provided in Your Profile:

- Location — free-text locality (e.g., 'Andheri West, Mumbai') (optional)
- About / Bio — up to 500 characters (optional)
- Social media profile links (optional)

### c) Data Provided When Creating a Service Listing:

- Service title, category, and description
- Contact phone numbers for the service (publicly visible to all users including unauthenticated visitors)
- Service location: city name and area/locality
- GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) — collected ONLY if you click the 'Use Current Location' button. These coordinates are stored in our database and are publicly visible on your service listing. You may enter your location manually as free text to avoid sharing GPS coordinates.
- Service availability, languages offered, and pricing

### d) Data from Google Sign-In (if used):

- If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google profile photo URL from Google LLC. These are stored in our database.

### e) Data Collected Automatically (with your explicit consent only):

- Page URLs visited, navigation events, and pageviews
- Device type, operating system, and browser
- Approximate geographic location at city/country level (derived from IP address by analytics providers)
- Session duration, engagement time, and referrer source
- Anonymised IP address

This data is collected ONLY after you explicitly accept cookies via our cookie consent banner. If you decline, no analytics data is collected.

### f) Data We Do NOT Collect:

- We do not collect Aadhaar numbers, PAN numbers, or any government-issued identity documents
- We do not collect profile photos (avatars are initials-based only)
- We do not process any payment information
- We do not collect biometric data of any kind
- We do not collect financial information such as bank account or card details

## 5. Purpose of Data Collection

In compliance with Rule 5(3) of the SPDI Rules, we collect personal data only for lawful purposes directly connected with the functioning of the Platform. The purposes are:

- To create, verify, and maintain your user account
- To display your profile and service listings to other users and visitors

- To enable ratings and reviews between users
- To maintain security, prevent fraud, and protect users
- To improve the Platform using privacy-respecting, consent-gated analytics
- To comply with applicable Indian laws and legal obligations

Your data will not be used for any purpose other than those stated above. Any change in purpose will be communicated to you with a fresh consent request.

## 7. Analytics Tools

We use two analytics tools, both disabled by default and activated only with your explicit cookie consent:

### a) Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — operated by Google LLC, USA:

- Activated only when you click 'Accept Cookies'
- Collects: pageviews, session data, device and browser information, approximate location (city/country), referrer, and anonymised IP address
- Sets cookies: \_ga and \_ga_XXXXXX (2-year expiry)
- Data is transferred to and processed by Google in the USA. By accepting cookies, you consent to this cross-border transfer.
- Google's processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy

### b) PostHog — operated by PostHog Inc., USA:

- Disabled by default (opt_out_capturing_by_default: true). Activated only when you click 'Accept Cookies'.
- Collects: pageviews with full URL, page-leave events, device type, browser, OS, screen resolution, referrer, and IP address
- Traffic is routed through our own domain (gullygig.in/ingest) before reaching PostHog's servers
- Data is transferred to and processed by PostHog in the USA. By accepting cookies, you consent to this cross-border transfer.
- PostHog's processing is governed by PostHog's Privacy Policy at posthog.com/privacy

## 8. Cookies and Local Storage

### a) Essential Cookies (always active, no consent required):

- Supabase authentication session cookies (pattern: sb-<project-ref>-auth-token) — HttpOnly and Secure. Required to keep you logged in. Access tokens expire in 1 hour; refresh tokens expire in 7 days.

### b) Preference Storage (localStorage — not HTTP cookies):

- cookie-consent — stores your preference ('accepted' or 'declined')
- rememberLoginPreference — stores your 'Keep me logged in' selection

### c) Analytics Cookies (only if you accept cookies):

- Google Analytics: \_ga, \_ga_XXXXXX (2-year expiry)
- PostHog: ph\_\* entries in cookies and localStorage for anonymous user identification

## 9. Third-Party Data Processors

In compliance with the SPDI Rules and the DPDP Act, we disclose all third parties that process your data on our behalf:

- Supabase (supabase.co, USA) — database and authentication backend. All user profile data, service listings, reviews, likes, and platform analytics are stored on Supabase servers. Data is transferred outside India to Supabase's infrastructure.
- Vercel (USA) — hosting and CDN. All HTTP requests to gullygig.in pass through Vercel's infrastructure. IP addresses are processed per Vercel's Privacy Policy.
- Google LLC (USA) — for Google Sign-In and Google Analytics 4 (consent-gated). Google Fonts loaded from fonts.googleapis.com may process your IP address on page load.
- PostHog Inc. (USA) — product analytics (consent-gated only).
- Cloudflare Inc. (USA) — DNS management and domain security for gullygig.in.
- OpenStreetMap Nominatim (nominatim.openstreetmap.org) — reverse geocoding service used when you click the GPS button. Your GPS coordinates are sent to this service to generate a readable address. Subject to OpenStreetMap's usage policy.
- BigDataCloud (api.bigdatacloud.net) — reverse geocoding fallback. Your GPS coordinates may be sent to this service if Nominatim is unavailable.

Cross-border data transfers: Several of our Data Processors are located in the USA. By using the Platform and accepting our Terms, you consent to the transfer of your personal data to these processors, which maintain data protection standards consistent with applicable Indian law. We have contractual arrangements with our Data Processors requiring them to implement appropriate security safeguards.

We do not use any email marketing service, SMS gateway, push notification service, or payment gateway. No financial data is processed by us or any third party on our behalf.

## 10. Public Visibility Warning

The following data is publicly visible to ALL visitors of the Platform, including unauthenticated users:

- Your full name and profile location text

## 11. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, in accordance with the data storage limitation principle:

- Account and profile data: retained while your account is active and for 2 years after account deletion
- Service listing data: retained while the listing is active and for 1 year after deletion
- Review and rating data: retained for 2 years
- Transaction and activity logs: retained as required by applicable Indian law
- Analytics data: governed by Google's and PostHog's respective retention policies

Upon expiry of the retention period, personal data will be deleted or anonymised. You may request early deletion by contacting support@gullygig.in.

## 12. Security Practices

In compliance with Rule 8 of the SPDI Rules and Section 8(5) of the DPDP Act, we implement the following reasonable security practices:

- All passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing — never stored in plain text
- All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Authentication sessions use HttpOnly and Secure cookies managed by Supabase Auth
- Access to data is controlled through Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies
- Access to production systems is restricted to authorised team members only

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to affect your rights, we will notify affected users and report to the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In) within 6 hours as mandated under Section 70B of the IT Act, 2000 and the CERT-In guidelines of 2022. We will also notify the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI) within 72 hours as required under the DPDP Act, 2023 once those provisions come into full effect.

## 13. Your Rights as a Data Principal

Under the SPDI Rules and the DPDP Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

- Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- Right to Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data. You can update most data directly in your profile settings.
- Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your account and personal data by emailing support@gullygig.in. Note: a self-serve account deletion feature is not currently available in the Platform interface.
- Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw consent for analytics at any time by clearing your browser cookies and localStorage. You may withdraw consent for data processing by requesting account deletion.
- Right to Grievance Redressal: You may raise a complaint with our Grievance Officer (see Section 2). If unresolved within 30 days, you may escalate to the Data Protection Board of India.
- Right to Nominate: Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity. To nominate, please contact support@gullygig.in.
